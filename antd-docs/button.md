## Overview

To trigger an operation.

### When to use

A button means an operation (or a series of operations). Clicking a button will trigger its corresponding business logic.

In Ant Design we provide 5 types of button.

- ًں”µ Primary button: used for the main action, there can be at most one primary button in a section.
- âڑھï¸ڈ Default button: used for a series of actions without priority.
- ًںک¶ Dashed button: commonly used for adding more actions.
- ًں”¤ Text button: used for the most secondary action.
- ًں”— Link button: used for external links.

And 4 other properties additionally.

- ًں”´ `danger`: used for actions of risk, like deletion or authorization.
- ًں‘» `ghost`: used in situations with complex background, home pages usually.
- ًںڑ« `disabled`: used when actions are not available.
- ًں”ƒ `loading`: adds a loading spinner in button, avoids multiple submits too.
